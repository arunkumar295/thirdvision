Thirdvision Benefits and Computer Vision Advantages

Thirdvision, an industry-first data-centric artificial intelligence (AI) computer vision software platform, helps improve model accuracy and reduce false rejection. The end-to-end platform standardizes deep learning solutions that reduce development time and scale projects easily to multiple facilities across the globe.

Increase Product Quality and Reduce Waste

Automated inspection is widely deployed to increase product quality and reduce waste by catching defects early in the manufacturing process. With Thirdvision, deep learning can make simple defect detection faster and easier to deploy, and in many cases applications that are very challenging because of the subjective nature of the features might be addressed with deep learning where other techniques cannot be successful.

Optimize Production Efficiency

With Thirdvision, users can build their own deep learning models and optimize inspection accuracy without any impact on production speed. Users can also easily incorporate deep learning solutions into existing machine vision solutions.

Get More Out of Your Data

Inconsistent data results in ineffective systems, and many companies spend more time trying to fix the model than improving the data. Thirdvision is designed for more effective data management and allows users to reach a consensus on defect definitions and analyze errors to build better solutions.

Faster Market Introduction

One of the benefits of computer vision is that Thirdvision reduces development time from months to weeks. The step-by-step user interface simplifies labeling and the training and deploying of models, which helps teams focus on tasks critical to building solutions without deeper technical knowledge. A shorter development process means a faster market introduction.

Join the Revolution

Adopt Changes More Easily

Environmental changes, such as changes in lighting, can impact your model. You can create alerts to stay up to date on any changes. You can then quickly add these images to your dataset, label them, and retrain your model. Iterating your model allows you to improve and build better models.

Join the Revolution

Standardize Workflow and Centralize Project Management

Thirdvision provides a centralized project management tool by allowing teams to collaborate among quality managers, subject matter experts, labelers, and developers to create better models. Meanwhile, a project leader can manage different model versions, project status, and results, monitoring all active development and deployed projects in one place.
